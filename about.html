Aims

Art is one of the entertainment of human's life. When I was a volunteer of teaching children how to draw, I created a theme : Pattern. This theme has fixed in three topics, Circle , Square and Triangle. By providing some fixed patterns not only can raise the memories of us, but also our creativity. Just spend some time to draw what you want with some easy shapes and lines, which is a good education to all of us. Even not all of us are professional painters, we all have potential of casual drawing.
